Cones of Confusion
Cone of confusion: A cone of directions around the interaural axis that produce identical ITD values.

Sources on the same cone are ambiguous using ITD/ILD alone. The brain resolves this via:
• Pinna (ear shape) filtering
• Head movement
• Visual cues
• Prior expectation
